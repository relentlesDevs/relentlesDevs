Use of Software

Autofuse is a software that calculates

  • Time taken by a pesticide to decompose to a certain mass
  • Ammount of pesticide left in a given timeframe
  • Rate at which a certain pesticide or chemical dissociates
    •

    Measurements

    SI units used for

  • Time are days (d)
  • Ammounts are grammes (g)
  • Dissociation constant are grammes lost per day (g/d)
    •

    Errors

    There may be possible

  • Rounding off errors
  • Systmatic errors
  • All measurements are averages
